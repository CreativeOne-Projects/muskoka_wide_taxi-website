About Us

Your Muskoka Ride,
Any Time.

Muskoka Wide Taxi is your trusted local transportation provider throughout cottage country. Whether you're heading to the dock, the airport, a dinner, or a medical appointment — we'll get you there safely and on time.

Our fleet comfortably supports groups of all sizes and our professionally trained drivers bring real local knowledge to every trip. Available whenever you need us, service that spans the entire District of Muskoka, and a genuine commitment to our community — every time.
